Technology is the present not the future

As a software developer, as a Programmer and most importantly as a human being,
I want to to tell you that technology is not just AI and that we don't only have to
look at the negative side such as machine learning and artificial intelligence overruling
humans but we must also look at the positive side. Technology has no age limit, no prerequisites,
it is often used for healthcare anyone can learn "even a 5 year old". It has made it easier for economies
to grow, for people to learn and travel. Technology should not just be the future it should also be the present.
